Category Editor



Using this editor you can add or remove departments and resources.

Removing Choice: to remove an option simply click on the department/resource you want to remove.

Adding Choice: to add an option simply click on the corresponding blue button.

NOTE: removing an option will remove that option from the choices in the assignment form.

Removing departments will cause projects with that department to change to an "Unknown" department, while removing a resource will remove that resource from all projects.
